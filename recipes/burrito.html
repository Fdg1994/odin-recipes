The Burrito Recipe

Description

Delicious beef burritos with various peppers and seasonings with refried beans.

Finish them off with sour cream or whatever!

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces sliced jalapeno peppers
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons hot sauce
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (1 ounce) package burrito seasoning
  • 1 (14 ounce) can refried beans
  • 6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 (10 ounce) bag shredded lettuce
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
